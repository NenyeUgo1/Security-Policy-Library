# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose
To ensure timely detection, reporting, and response to security incidents.

## Requirements
- All employees must report incidents immediately.
- The IR team must classify incidents within 1 hour.
- Major incidents require executive notification.
- Post‑incident reviews must be completed within 10 days.

## References
ISO 27001 A.16, NIST RS, SOC 2 CC7.
