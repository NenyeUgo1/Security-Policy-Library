# Access Control Policy

## 1. Purpose
To ensure that access to information systems is granted based on business need, least privilege, and segregation of duties.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, systems, applications, and data assets.

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities
- Security Team — defines access standards and monitors compliance.
- System Owners — approve access requests.
- Managers — validate business justification.
- Users — maintain secure credentials and follow access rules.

## 4. Policy Requirements
- Access must be granted based on least privilege.
- MFA is required for all systems handling sensitive data.
- Access reviews must be performed quarterly.
- Accounts must be disabled within 24 hours of termination.
- Shared accounts are prohibited.

## 5. Exceptions
Exceptions must be documented and approved by the Security Team.

## 6. Compliance & Enforcement
Non‑compliance may result in disciplinary action.

## 7. Review & Maintenance
Reviewed annually or after major system changes.

## 8. References
ISO 27001 A.9, NIST PR.AC, SOC 2 CC6.
