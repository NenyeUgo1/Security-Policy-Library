# Data Classification Policy

## 1. Purpose
To ensure information is classified and protected according to sensitivity and regulatory requirements.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all data created, processed, stored, or transmitted by the organization.

## 3. Classification Levels
- Public  
- Internal  
- Confidential  
- Highly Confidential  

## 4. Handling Requirements
- Highly Confidential data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Confidential data must not be shared externally without approval.
- Public data may be freely distributed.

## 5. Responsibilities
- Data Owners classify data.
- Users follow handling rules.

## 6. Review & Maintenance
Annual review.

## 7. References
ISO 27001 A.8, GDPR, NIST PR.DS.
