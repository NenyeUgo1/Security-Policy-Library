# Vendor Risk Management Policy

## Purpose
To ensure third‑party vendors are assessed, monitored, and managed based on risk.

## Requirements
- All vendors must undergo a risk assessment before onboarding.
- High‑risk vendors require annual reassessment.
- Contracts must include security and privacy clauses.
- Vendors must notify the organization of security incidents.

## References
ISO 27001 A.15, NIST ID.SC, SOC 2 CC3.
